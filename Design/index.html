Our Story

Two Decades of
Crafting Beauty

Founded in 1998 by architect Elara Voss, Interior Studio began as a small practice with a bold vision: that good design should be both beautiful and deeply personal.

Over the past 27 years, we've grown into a team of 45 specialists — architects, lighting designers, furniture curators, and project managers — united by a single passion for transforming spaces.

We've completed over 840 projects across 30 countries, from private residences to luxury hotels and flagship retail spaces, each one a unique story told in space and light.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a typical interior design project take?+
Project timelines vary based on scope. A single room typically takes 4–6 weeks from concept to completion. A full home redesign generally takes 3–6 months. We'll give you a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.
Do you work with clients outside of Mumbai?+
Absolutely. We work with clients across India and internationally. For remote projects, we leverage our 3D visualization tools and virtual consultations to deliver the same quality experience. Travel can be arranged for key milestones.
What is your minimum budget requirement?+
Our Essential package starts at ₹75,000 per room for design-only services. Full home packages begin around ₹1.8 lakh. For procurement and execution projects, we work with budgets starting at ₹10 lakh for a full home.
Can I be involved in the design process?+
Absolutely — your involvement is the heart of our process. We start with an in-depth discovery session to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and goals. You'll have regular review touchpoints throughout, and we welcome your feedback at every stage.
Do you handle construction and renovation work?+
We don't perform construction ourselves, but through our Bespoke package we manage the entire process — vetting contractors, overseeing quality, managing timelines and budgets. Think of us as your dedicated project concierge.
What styles do you specialise in?+
We have deep expertise in Scandinavian-minimalist, contemporary luxury, Japandi, mid-century modern, and transitional styles. That said, our strongest skill is listening to YOU — we don't impose a house style, we create your style.
How do I get started?+
Simply fill out our contact form or call us. We'll schedule a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to learn about your project. From there, we'll recommend the right package and send you a detailed proposal.
